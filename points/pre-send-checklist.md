# Pre-send checklist

Run this before hitting send on any cold email, op-ed, pitch, memo, or LinkedIn post. If you can't tick every relevant box, you're not done.

## Universal (every piece)

- [ ] **Main point** ‚Äî can I state it in one sentence? Is it in the first sentence?
- [ ] **Audience** ‚Äî who specifically is reading this? What do they want to feel?
- [ ] **BLUF** ‚Äî is the bottom line up front, or am I burying it under organ music?
- [ ] **Length** ‚Äî is this as short as it can be without losing substance? Cut 20% more.
- [ ] **Sentence rhythm** ‚Äî do sentences vary in length? Read aloud ‚Äî does it have music?
- [ ] **Tone** ‚Äî would I say this at dinner with a friend? Conversational, not stiff.
- [ ] **Like-and-would-like** ‚Äî if I'm critiquing, did I name what's working before what isn't?
- [ ] **Warmth** ‚Äî is there at least one moment that makes the reader smile?
- [ ] **Front-page test** ‚Äî would I be okay with this on the front page of the NYT?
- [ ] **Scannability** ‚Äî bold, bullets, white space where helpful?
- [ ] **Names and details** ‚Äî every name, firm, title spelled correctly? Checked twice?
- [ ] **Read aloud** ‚Äî sounds like me, not like ChatGPT?

## Cold email

- [ ] **Subject line** ‚Äî personal, timely, unusual? Would *they* open it?
- [ ] **First sentence** ‚Äî tells them something they don't know, not what they already know about themselves?
- [ ] **Like you** ‚Äî specific and genuine, not generic or self-diminishing?
- [ ] **Story** ‚Äî one vivid scene with a date, place, line of dialogue, or image?
- [ ] **How you help them** ‚Äî specifically what I can do for them, not what I want?
- [ ] **Secret about the future** ‚Äî one insight or thesis they haven't heard?
- [ ] **Ask** ‚Äî small, specific, easy to say yes to? Door open for no?
- [ ] **Offer** ‚Äî am I giving something, not just taking?
- [ ] **Length** ‚Äî under 200 words?
- [ ] **Right person** ‚Äî not `jobs@` or `pitches@`? Actual human?
- [ ] **Sign-off** ‚Äî has personality (not "Best" or "Sincerely")?

## Op-ed

- [ ] **Why me** ‚Äî what makes me uniquely qualified? Was I in the room? Lived experience?
- [ ] **Why now** ‚Äî what makes this timely? News hook?
- [ ] **Audience and publication** ‚Äî which one am I writing for?
- [ ] **Cinematic opener** ‚Äî real person, vivid scene, specific details?
- [ ] **Thesis** ‚Äî is it bold and arguable, not a neutral description?
- [ ] **Real interviews** ‚Äî at least one person interviewed in person or on Zoom (not just web)?
- [ ] **Best counterargument** ‚Äî refuted, not strawmanned?
- [ ] **Solution** ‚Äî am I offering one, or just complaining?
- [ ] **Headline** ‚Äî bold, arguable claim that makes the reader want to argue back?
- [ ] **Length** ‚Äî 500‚Äì800 words?
- [ ] **Co-author** ‚Äî would adding a recognized expert dramatically increase publication odds?

## Pitch

- [ ] **Six-word summary** ‚Äî can I describe the product in six words?
- [ ] **Plain English** ‚Äî would my grandmother understand what we do?
- [ ] **Differentiation** ‚Äî clear vs. existing solutions?
- [ ] **Specific customers** ‚Äî names, numbers, real endorsements?
- [ ] **Superhero story** ‚Äî why am I the one to do this?
- [ ] **News hook** ‚Äî why now?
- [ ] **Bigger trend** ‚Äî what wave does this connect to?
- [ ] **S.H.I.T.** ‚Äî Simple, Heroic, Inevitable, Timely?

## Gratitude note

- [ ] **Cinematic** ‚Äî specific moment, date, place visualized?
- [ ] **Impact** ‚Äî using "even now" language about how it shaped me?
- [ ] **Recipient** ‚Äî am I writing to someone who rarely gets thanks?
- [ ] **Emotion** ‚Äî am I writing while I feel it, not weeks later?
- [ ] **Handwritten** ‚Äî could I send this on paper instead of email?
- [ ] **No AI** ‚Äî every word is mine?

## Jargon scrub (always)

Cut on sight:

- [ ] `currently`, `synergy`, `leverage`, `align`, `drive`, `strategize`
- [ ] `empower`, `enable`, `deliverables`, `utilize`, `incentivize`, `facilitate`
- [ ] `impact` (use harm/benefits)
- [ ] *"at the intersection of [X, Y, Z]"*
- [ ] *"I hope this email finds you well"*, *"In today's fast-paced world"*
- [ ] *"It's not just X ‚Äî it's Y"* (AI tell)
- [ ] *"Delve into"*, *"Tapestry of"*, *"Navigate the complexities"*
- [ ] *"Just following up"* ‚Äî write a new email instead

## Final pass

- [ ] **Read aloud** ‚Äî every sentence
- [ ] **One specific detail** only I would know ‚Äî added?
- [ ] **Sign-off** ‚Äî has personality?
- [ ] **Recipient name** ‚Äî spelled correctly? Their firm? Their title?
- [ ] **Attachments** ‚Äî actually attached?
- [ ] **Subject line** ‚Äî last edit before sending?

When in doubt: **send to yourself first.** Read it on your phone. Cut anything that feels like filler.
