Draft Critic Coach

Winning Writing — Coach

Tell Claude who you're writing to and what you want. It researches the recipient, finds genuine connections to you, drafts the email, and scores it. Three fields, one button.

1Who are you emailing?

A name plus role or company is enough. Coach searches the web for the rest — LinkedIn, podcasts, recent posts, news.

2What do you want?

One sentence. Coach figures out the rest — why-now, ask size, how to frame the offer.

3About you strongly recommended

Brief Claude on who you are. Persists in your browser between runs.

Pro tip: drop a context/about-me.md file in your repo (template included) and click Load from file. Every session writes in the same voice — no retyping.

Optional: existing draft

If you have one, paste it. Coach critiques line-by-line, then rewrites. Leave empty to draft from scratch.

One Sonnet call (~$0.02, ~8s). Returns highlighted spans with Accept / Reject.

Inline coach

Hover any highlight for the rule and suggested fix. Click Accept to apply the suggestion to your working draft, Reject to keep the original, or Snooze to hide it for this session. The original draft is preserved until you click Apply to draft input.

Run the coach

Stored only in your browser's localStorage. Sent only to api.anthropic.com. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

Pipeline trace

Each step is one Claude call. Click any step to inspect its output. This is the auditable agentic path — the same flow Claude Code runs in your terminal, just visible.

Output

Fill in the three fields above, then click Run the coach. Claude's full pipeline output appears here, dossier, connection angles, subject lines, the email, scored rubric.

Runs the same Sonnet critic on the email Claude just produced.
