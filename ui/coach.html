Draft Critic (offline) Coach (Claude-powered)

Winning Writing — Coach

Tell Claude who you're writing to and what you want. It researches the recipient, finds genuine connections to you, drafts the email, and scores it. Three fields, one button.

1. Who are you emailing?

Just a name + role or company is enough. Coach searches the web for the rest — LinkedIn, podcasts, recent posts, blog, news.

2. What do you want?

In one sentence. Coach figures out the rest — why-now, the right ask size, how to frame the offer.

3. About you (strongly recommended)

Brief Claude on who you are — career, distinctive credentials, what you're building. Persists in your browser.

Pro tip: drop a context/about-me.md file in your repo (template included) and click Load from file. That way every session writes in the same voice — you don't have to retype.

Optional: existing draft

If you have one, paste it. Coach critiques line-by-line then rewrites. Leave empty to draft from scratch.

Claude API

Stored only in your browser's localStorage. Sent only to api.anthropic.com. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

Output

Fill in the three fields, then click "Run the coach." Claude's full pipeline output appears here.
